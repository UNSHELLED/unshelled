## What This Is

MAP.md lists **where the maps live** ‚Äî not the full hierarchy (that's `STRUCTURE.md`). Each subtree keeps its own **`INDEX.md`** ("what lives in this folder").

**Why not inside `.venom/`?** `.venom/` is **runtime brain** for a project. A map of the **entire** repo (platforms, consciousness, anatomy, ‚Ä¶) belongs **above** that layer.
