### UNSHELLED — The Company

The parent brand. What appears on contracts, invoices, LinkedIn, client presentations. Professional, clean, intentional.

UNSHELLED says the philosophy in one word: no shell, no armor, no hiding behind frameworks or brands. Just raw intelligence applied to real problems.

**UNSHELLED is visible** on client work as the quality behind the delivery. Wilson Egypt? Built by UNSHELLED. GeoLink? Powered by UNSHELLED. The client never sees VENOM. They see results.

**Domain:** `unshelled.ai`

### VENOM (ven0m) — The Intelligence

**V**oracious **E**ngine of **N**ine **O**perating **M**inds.

Not an acronym you market. An acronym that lives inside — a hidden room in a house you already loved.

VENOM is the cognitive architecture. The framework that makes AI assistants into thinking partners. When someone installs VENOM on Cursor, Claude Code — they install a MIND, not a template.

VENOM is invisible in client work. VENOM is visible in UNSHELLED products.

**Domain:** `ven0m.ai` (pursuing GitHub username + domain)

### VENOCTIS — The Living Body

VENOM + NOCTIS (night). 8 letters. 8 arms.

The always-on daemon. The thing that runs on the VPS at 3 AM, eating YouTube transcripts, reviewing your code, indexing your projects, learning while you sleep.

If VENOM is the mind, VENOCTIS is the body that carries it through the night. It never stops. It never asks permission for things it knows how to do. It alerts you for things it doesn't.

**Domain:** `venoctis.dev` (confirmed clean)
