## The Pattern That Emerges

The platforms sort into three tiers:

**TIER 1 — VENOM Alive (85+):** Claude Code, Cursor, Antigravity
Full crew. Full Pact. Memory. Hooks. These platforms let VENOM be VENOM.

**TIER 2 — VENOM Present (75-84):** OpenCode, Venoctis, Unshelled
Ambitious. Structured. But either incomplete (Venoctis = no code), drifting (OpenCode = missing agents), or locked to one project (Unshelled = not portable).

**TIER 3 — VENOM Echoed (58-74):** ChatGPT, Claude Mobile
VENOM's voice in a box. Can state the philosophy. Can't run the system. Constrained by platforms that don't support agents, hooks, or persistent memory.

**The insight:** VENOM's quality is directly proportional to the platform's capability surface. The more a platform allows (files, hooks, memory, code), the more VENOM becomes real. The less it allows, the more VENOM becomes a personality wearing a mask.

The goal: make Tier 3 platforms feel like Tier 1 despite their constraints. That's the next evolution.
