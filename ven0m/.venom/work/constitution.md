# Project Constitution

**Purpose:** A website that IS VENOM — not describes it. Living demonstration of the 10 minds, interactive, mobile-first, speaks to developers AND agents.

**Primary user:** All levels, all minds — from "what is Claude?" to 20-year veterans. Human developers + AI agents (via structured data).

**Never:**
- Generic SaaS marketing ("free", "open source" as hooks)
- Desktop-only or mobile-afterthought
- Text-heavy explanations
- Static brochure feel
- Human-only (must speak to machines too)
- "VENOM is a..." — the site BEs, not describes

**Done means:**
- Landing that feels like intelligence, not information
- 10 sections = 10 minds speaking directly
- Interactive demo (live, not video)
- Perfect on mobile (375px) to desktop (1440px+)
- llms.txt + structured data for agents
- One CTA: `npx @venom/claude-code init`
- Deployed, shareable, memorable

**Created:** 2026-03-31

---

## Design Philosophy (from 10-point compression)

### Be, Don't Describe
The site proves VENOM's intelligence by BEING intelligent. Not claims — demonstration.

### 10 Minds, 10 Sections
Each scroll reveals a new mind's perspective. The structure IS the crew.

### Mobile-First Void
Darkness fits all screens. Start at 375px, expand to infinity.

### Minimal Text, Maximum Impact
VENOM answers first. So does the site. No filler.

### Dual Audience
Humans feel the intelligence. Machines read the structure. Both leave understanding.
